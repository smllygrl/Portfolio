ABOUT

Happy tidings dear reader! I'm Natalie, and I'm changing careers! I have spent 8 years in the education sector, and I'm ecstatic to be accelerating into technology.

My passion for education and technology both come from the same idea: equitable futures. Technology is becoming increasingly ubiquitous, and it's my intention to have both the hard and soft skills to make meaningful change in teams, businesses and society. My career to date has refined my ability to design accessible, relevant and meaningful structures for people to interact with.

In my personal life, I am a cat parent to two beautiful fur babies. I grew up on Millowl, the land of the Yallok Bulluk people (Phillip Island), and now reside in Naarm (Melbourne). I love the art scene in this city, and am often at live music venues, art galleries and museums! I also unforunately do improv comedy.
